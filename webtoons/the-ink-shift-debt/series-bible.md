# THE INK-SHIFT DEBT — Series Bible

## Logline
In a world where your birthmark is your destiny, a carefree forest-dweller with a "living" ink stain must hunt down a syndicate of tattoo-thieves to pay off his master's impossible debts.

## Magic System

### Golden Scripts (The Academy)
- Permanent geometric birthmarks that glow gold
- "Finished Magic" — one spell per mark, trained to be harder/faster
- Predictable: if script is on right arm, attack comes from right

### Living Ink (Tzubaki's "Shift")
- Messy black Ink-Blot that moves across his skin
- Three Hearts change how the world behaves:
  - **Iron Heart (Solid):** Unbreakable. Visual: Black metallic sheen
  - **Flowing Heart (Liquid):** Ignores impact, flows around obstacles. Visual: Rippling water-like ink
  - **Ghost Heart (Ethereal):** Weightless shadow, passes through Fixed magic. Visual: Fading grey smoke

## Factions

### The Academy
- Teaches Golden Script mages
- Secretly buying stolen marks from Harvesters for noble children born without talent

### The Harvesters
- Rogue "Unmarked" organization
- Peel Golden Scripts off living mages using forbidden Siphon tools
- **Chimeras:** High-rankers with multiple grafted stolen marks
- Building a "perfect mage" from stolen parts

### The Hermit (Tzubaki's Master)
- Former Grand Archivist of the Academy
- Discovered the Academy-Harvester collusion
- Stole the "Source Ink" (Tzubaki) and fled
- Tzubaki now carries the debt for all missing marks

## Tzubaki's Personality
- **The Minimalist:** Never uses more energy than needed
- **The Analyst:** Reads Golden Scripts like "code," finds structural weaknesses
- **The Debt-Obsessed:** Will stop mid-fight to calculate property damage costs

## Episode Summaries

### Ep 1: The Zero-Sum Game
- Caught eating illegal phase-shifted berries in the forest
- Brought to Academy in chains
- Put in cage with captured Harvester to prove worth
- Uses Ghost Heart to ignore gravity, un-peels stolen tattoo with one touch
- Headmistress offers the Scholarship/Debt deal

### Ep 2: Class F and the "Blank" Victim
- Joins the Basement Class, meets Husk roommate (mark stolen)
- Motivation shifts from debt to actually helping people
- Comedy: draws fake tattoo on roommate with Flowing Heart, it melts into a puddle at a fancy party

### Ep 3: The Chimera's Ambush
- Harvester Collector attacks school festival
- Four arms, four stolen combat scripts
- Elite students' static shields shattered
- Tzubaki shifts scarf into Iron Heart (staff), fights like a pro
- Finisher: Ghost Heart bypasses armor, Flowing Heart liquefies stolen tattoos into collection jars
